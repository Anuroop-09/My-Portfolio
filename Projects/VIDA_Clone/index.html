VIDA is a precision lung health company, accelerating therapies to patients through AI-powered lung intelligence. VIDA Discovery services enable more efficient respiratory clinical trials by providing more precise quantitative endpoints, AI-powered data quality controls, site training, and more to help biopharma sponsors save millions in drug development costs.
